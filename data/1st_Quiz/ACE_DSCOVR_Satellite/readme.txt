#Data Format

day			: Number of day
time			: Time of day
proton_density		: Proton Density (particle/(cm)^3).
proton_temperature	: Radial Component of proton temperature (deg. Kelvin).
proton_speed		: Proton Speed (km/s)
bx_gsm    		: X-component of mag. field in GSM (nT).
by_gsm    		: Y-component of mag. field in GSM (nT).
bz_gsm    		: Z-component of mag. field in GSM (nT).
bt        		: <|B|> magnetic field magnitude (nT).
A value of nan indicates bad or missing data.
